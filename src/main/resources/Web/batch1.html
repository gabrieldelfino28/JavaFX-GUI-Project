Batch 1 - Decision Structure

Receive the amount of hours worked, the amount per hour, the percentage discount and the number of descendants. Calculate the salary that will be the hours worked x the value per hour.
Calculate the net salary (= Salary Gross – discount). To each dependent will be added R $ 100 in Salary Liquid. View the salary to be received.

Calculate the amount of liters spent on a trip, knowing that the car makes 12 km / l. Receive the travel time and average speed.

Receive 2 integer values. Calculate and show the result of the difference of the higher at least value.

Receive 2 real values. Calculate and show the largest of them.

Get 3 coefficients A, B, and C from a 2nd degree equation of the formula AX²+BX+C=0. Check and show the existence of real roots and if case exist, calculate and show.

Receive 4 bimonthly grades from a student. Calculate and show the arithmetic mean. Show the message according to the average:
a. If the average is >= 6.0 display "APPROVED";
b. If the mean is >= 3.0 or < 6.0 display "EXAM";
c. If the average is < 3.0 display "RETAINED".

Receive 2 different integer values. Show your values in order crescent.

Receive 3 values obligatorily in ascending order and a 4th value not necessarily in order. Show the 4 numbers in ascending order.

Receive an integer amount. Check and show if it is divisible by 2 and 3.

Receive the start and end time of a game (HH,MM) knowing that the Maximum time is 24 hours and can start on one day and end on another.

Get 2 integer values. Check and show if the largest number is multiple of the smallest.

Receive the number of laps, the length of the circuit (in meters) and the time of duration (minutes). Calculate and show the average speed in km/h.

Receive the current price and monthly average of a product. Calculate and show the New price knowing that:
Monthly Sale | Current Price | New Price
< 500 | < 30 | + 10%
>= 500 and < 1000 | >= 30 and < 80 | +15%
>= 1000 | >= 80 | - 5%
Note: for other conditions, the new price will be the same as the current price.

Receive the type of investment (1 = savings and 2 = fixed income) and the value of the investment. Calculate and show the corrected value in 30 days knowing that the savings = 3% and fixed income = 5%. Other types will not be considered.

Receive the date of birth and current in year, month and day. Calculate and show the age in years, months and days, considering leap years.
